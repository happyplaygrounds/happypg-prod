Playground Safety Surfacing

With 70% of playground injuries resulting from falls onto unforgiving surfaces, how you handle surfacing is very important for both child safety and effective risk management. It goes without saying that the surface under and around your playground equipment is of critical importance. Today, there are many choices that offer protection from falls that are compliant with modern standards, both loose-fill or unitary options, across a variety of aesthetic and playful effects.

We offer multiple types of safety surfacing material that will complement your design and stay within your budget. Allow us to help you explore the many options in playground surfacing. Count on us to provide valuable tools to help you increase the play value of your surface and functionality of the play space. Contact us for more info on each of our surfacing lines!

Zeager Bros, Inc.

Safer Starts from the Ground Up.



The surface beneath your playground is just as important as the equipment above it. Falls are the leading cause of playground injuries, and choosing the right surfacing can significantly reduce risk while supporting inclusive, accessible play for all. While grass or packed earth might appear soft, they quickly become compacted, uneven, and slippery—offering little to no real fall protection. These surfaces don't meet ASTM safety standards and can increase the risk of injury from even low-height falls. That’s where Zeager Brothers comes in. Their engineered wood fiber (EWF) is purpose-built to absorb impact, promote drainage, and stay consistent over time. Both options meet critical safety and accessibility requirements, offering peace of mind for schools, parks, and communities.With Zeager, you're not just covering the ground—you’re laying the groundwork for safer, more inclusive play.

IMC Outdoor Living

Smarter Surfaces. Greener Futures.



IMC Outdoor Living turns yesterday’s tires into tomorrow’s playgrounds. While shredded rubber mulch may not look traditional, it’s one of the most sustainable surfacing solutions available. By recycling used tires, IMC diverts thousands of pounds of waste from landfills and transforms it into safe, durable, and low-maintenance ground cover for playgrounds, landscapes, and recreational areas.
