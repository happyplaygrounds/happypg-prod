Shelters and Pavilions

"Our line of shelters and pavilions offer a variety of styles that will compliment your space and work within your budget." Kelli Collins

Outdoor shelters and pavilions allow families and friends to gather together to enjoy cookouts, birthday parties, reunions, and more. Not only do they serve a functional purpose, but they are also aesthetically pleasing.

Our shelters are manufactured by Americana Outdoors, and we would love to help you add one to your park. Americana has a shelter that is perfect for you and your budget.

OUR PURPOSE and VISION

Leaders who serve and influence our community need a trusted partner who believes in their mission and can co-create venues to achieve it. It’s why we exist. Our vision is to be known as the outdoor recreation leader that advocates for the foundation of a happier, healthier community. We want to provide our communities with a place to play in venues we’ve helped build.
