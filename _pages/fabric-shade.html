Outdoor Shade Structures

We all know that too much sun exposure is not a healthy choice for anyone. However, high temperatures and sunshine should not keep us from enjoying the outdoors! With the proper shade structures, you can reduce the temperature  significantly and protect your skin from harmful UV rays.


For the past 25 years, USA Shade has been the proven leader in full-service, design-and- build commercial shade manufacturers. Located in Dallas, USA Shade works closely with Happy Playgrounds to provide your recreational space with a shade that is perfect and beautiful. Just for you.
