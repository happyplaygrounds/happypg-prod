Poured in Place Outdoor Maintenance Recommendations

Monthly Maintenance

Utilize a leaf blower, broom, or water hose to clear the surface of any loose debris. Examples of debris includes leaves, mud, gum, etc.

Stains

The stained area to be cleaned must be dampened before the cleaning solution is applied. Apply a full strength all purpose cleanser using a spray bottle. Scrub with medium basine bristle brush. Repeat if the stain is difficult to remove.

AVOID THE FOLLOWING CHEMICALS

  • Chlorine Bleach
  • Concentrated Degreasers
  • Gasoline
  • Diesel
  • Solvents
  • Disinfectants
  • Thinners
