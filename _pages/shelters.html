Shelters and Pavilions

"Our line of shelters and pavilions offer a variety of styles that will complement your space and work within your budget." Kelli Collins

Outdoor shelters and pavilions allow families and friends to gather together to enjoy cookouts, birthday parties, reunions, and more.

These structures are essential features in parks, playgrounds, and public spaces, offering year-round protection from the elements and a central place for people to gather, rest, and enjoy the outdoors. From school campuses to city parks and trailheads, these structures provide shaded comfort for picnics, events, or quiet relaxation—while also enhancing the site’s visual appeal and usability. Whether it’s protecting families from the sun during a summer afternoon or providing a dry space for outdoor learning and recreation, shelters and pavilions are practical investments that bring lasting value to any community.

At Happy Playgrounds, we offer a wide selection of high-quality shelters and pavilions in a variety of styles, sizes, and finishes to suit your unique space. Our structures are engineered for durability, easy maintenance, and aesthetic appeal—designed to withstand the elements while complementing your site’s environment. Whether you're planning a school improvement project, a new park installation, or a community space upgrade, our team is here to guide you through the process, from concept to completion. Let Happy Playgrounds help you create a comfortable, welcoming space that brings people together.

Our shelters are manufactured by Americana Outdoors, and we would love to help you add one to your park. Americana offers shelters that fit your needs and budget perfectly.
