Wabash Valley

Built to Last. Designed to Impress.



Wabash Valley sets the standard for site furnishings that are as tough as they are timeless. From benches and tables to bike racks and trash receptacles, their products are built to withstand the elements—and heavy public use—without losing their clean, professional look. With a wide range of styles, finishes, and mounting options, Wabash Valley makes it easy to find the right fit for any space, from parks and schools to city streetscapes. If you're looking for long-lasting value and low-maintenance durability, Wabash delivers.
