Turnkey Installation for Playgrounds, Shade & Surfacing

At Happy Playgrounds, we do more than supply great products—we bring your entire recreation vision to life. From initial planning and site preparation to final installation and project closeout, we offer turnkey construction solutions for both public and private spaces. Whether you're building a new playground, adding shade structures, installing a shelter, or upgrading surfacing, our experienced team handles every step with precision and care.

We’re proud to be your one-stop partner for design, equipment, and professional installation—all under one roof. With Happy Playgrounds, you get a fully managed, start-to-finish process that delivers safe, functional, and beautiful spaces your community can enjoy for years to come.

Our installation services include:

  • Playground equipment installation
  • Shade structure and shelter assembly
  • Poured-in-place and turf surfacing systems
  • Site prep, grading, and concrete work
  • ADA accessibility and code compliance
  • Repairs, replacements, and retrofits
  • Project management from pre-construction through closeout
