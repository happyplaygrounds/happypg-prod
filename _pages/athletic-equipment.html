An unfortunate fact in America today involves the obesity rate. As of 2016, 37.9% of men, and 41.1% of women are obese. Cities and municipalities are aware of this and are actively doing their part to help combat this growing problem. Happy Playgrounds is pleased to have been involved in numerous outdoor athletic projects.
