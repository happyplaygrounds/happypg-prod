Happy Playgrounds' Manufacturers

Playworld delivers endless wonder and possibilities, from early childhood and school age play, to adolescent and adult equipment made for fitness and wellness. Creativity is king at Playworld, where we believe that play is vital to everyone’s health and well-being. Play is something you’re never too young or too old to enjoy. Our equipment is designed to unleash the power of play to grow stronger bodies and more imaginative minds. We deliver play experiences for all ages and abilities.

NF Surfaces has been the premier provider of innovative surfacing products for over 40 years. In that time we have installed over 15 million square feet of poured-in-place surface worldwide and have served more than 12,000 happy customers. We’ve kept over 9 million tires from littering landfills in the United States, and we continue to provide quality product installations by the most experienced and knowledgeable crews in the industry.

Soft Play offers contained, safe indoor play structures for any age, ability, space, and budget. Our exciting, challenging playgrounds are proven family attractions. Soft Play’s customized play options are created by the most experienced team in the industry. We’ve delivered indoor play experiences for quick-serve and full-serve restaurants, shopping malls, health and fitness clubs, auto dealers, amusement parks, grocery stores, and more.

PLAYTIME is the global provider of unique entertainment experiences for indoor, outdoor, and water locations. We’re experts in using immersive environments to engage the imagination, providing a distinctive play experience. PLAYTIME is a creative and design agency that has produced projects of all themes and styles in a variety of forms, within safe and stimulating environments. We utilize a turnkey process, from collaborative custom design to full build-out of unique and innovative play areas, maximizing the active play experience at your location.

Tayplay designs amazing rope play structures and climbing frames that empower children to safely climb to new heights. At Tayplay, we believe in playing and living actively through creative rope and net play systems. We bring challenge and fun to any facility. With more than 20 years’ experience in our industry, you can be confident in our quality, safe designs, and superior engineering.

USA SHADE & Fabric Structures provides full-service, design-build fabric shade structures that deliver visual distinction while protecting children and adults from the harmful effects of the sun’s UV rays. As the innovator and leader in fabric shade structure design and technology, we’ll exceed your requirements. We put people and creativity first to deliver functional, durable, and beautiful installations, custom-designed for every need.

Americana Outdoors specializes in creating elements for life outside: Shelters, Shade Structures, Sports Equipment, & Site Amenities. These different elements are handled by unique divisions of our company to provide the best customer experience. Americana Outdoors functions as a parent group and specializes in shade and shelter.

VPS is the pioneer and leader in the design and manufacture of covered parking systems. Our products protect people and valuable assets from hail, heat, and UV damage. As a design-build firm, we believe every design is unique, from single car lots to large auto assembly plants.

EZ Dock provides unlimited waterfront solutions, with easy-to-configure floating docks, ports, launches, and walkways. EZ Dock’s floating, durable, and eco-friendly dock systems withstand harsh weather and won’t chip, warp, or peel, giving you more time on the water. Our unique connecting system allows ultimate design flexibility for varying dock configurations, drive-on lifts, kayak launches, PWC ports, and water walkways. Easy to install and virtually maintenance free, it’s the last dock you will need.

Wabash Valley offers a large selection of tables, chairs, litter receptacles, planters, umbrellas and more, all available in numerous styles, colors and finishes to fit whatever your need—functional, elegant, eco-friendly, always reliable—without the high price. Under one roof, you’ll find an amazing variety of styles and colors in our diverse product lines.

For over 50 years, Cedar Forest Products (CFP) has been designing and creating pre-engineered structures for public and private parks, camps and recreation facilities. Hundreds of standard designs allow flexibility and customization for any requirement or budget. CFP buildings are low maintenance, designed to perform and built to last.
