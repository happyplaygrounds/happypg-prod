Full-Service Playground & Park Construction

At Happy Playgrounds, we don’t just install—we build from the ground up. Our full-service construction solutions cover every phase of your recreation project, from pre-construction planning to final walk-through. Whether you're working with a school, city, HOA, or private developer, we bring the experience and expertise to manage your project with precision, safety, and care.

We handle everything from site prep and concrete work to equipment installation, surfacing, and final detailing. As a true turnkey provider, we streamline the entire process—eliminating the need for multiple contractors and ensuring your project stays on time, on budget, and on spec.

Why choose Happy Playgrounds for construction?

  • Turnkey construction services for public and private projects
  • In-house project management from start to finish
  • Site grading, excavation, and foundational work
  • Concrete footings, pathways, and slab pours
  • Equipment assembly and secure anchoring
  • Compliance with ADA, CPSC, and local building codes
  • Fully licensed, insured, and safety-certified teams

Whether it’s a brand-new park or a major renovation, we build spaces where communities come to play— and memories are made.
