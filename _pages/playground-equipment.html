Inclusive Play

Are you interested in a playground that is genuinely inclusive for all people? One of our principles at Happy Playgrounds is designing a playground that truly everyone can engage in and that brings together people from every stretch of the community. Recreational areas need to be enjoyed by individuals with and without challenges to foster opportunities for engagement with one another. We have a framework that will promote that type of play. Our certified Inclusive Play Design Specialists are focused on bringing the ability to enjoy and share in the holistic benefits of play to everyone! Inclusion is more than access—it’s about being and feeling a part of something. It’s about making a difference in the lives of others. It's about happiness for all.

Duncan and Grove

Beautiful. Bold. Built from Nature.



Duncan & Grove doesn't just build playgrounds - they carve stories into wood. Specializing in custom robinia play environments, their work blends artistry with engineering to create nature-inspired spaces that feel as good as they look. Every piece of robinia is park tough and sustainably sourced. We prove that play can be both beautiful and bold.

Play Mart

Reimagining Play — Responsibly.



Play Mart redefines what sustainable play can look like—eco-conscious, inclusive, and built to last. Their equipment is crafted from recycled structural plastic lumber, offering the durability of traditional materials with a much smaller environmental footprint. Every design is thoughtfully made in the USA, with attention to accessibility, imaginative play, and real-world budgets. Play Mart delivers green solutions that don’t just meet standards, they raise them.
