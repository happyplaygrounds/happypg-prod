Engineered Wood Fiber Maintenance Recommendations

Monthly Maintenance

  • Inspect the area and remove any foreign objects on a weekly basis
  • Rake the EWF to ensure it is level.
  • Ensure the depth of the EWF measures to original specifications
  • Consider wear mats to reduce or eliminate the need to rake EWF in high traffic areas such as under swings or at slide exits
  • Engineered wood fiber may need to be topped off with fresh surfacing annually.
